Flexible flight bookings with Cash & Miles


With Cash & Miles you can redeem your award miles just as you wish, for example for a flight to your travel
destination of choice. This is possible from as little as 3,000 miles. At lufthansa.com you have the option of
redeeming your miles on all available flights.

    • What is Cash & Miles?




    With Cash & Miles, Miles & More members can redeem their award miles in part or in full for flights at
    lufthansa.com. This option is available for all travel classes. The fare is calculated based on the total ticket price,
    including taxes, fees, and surcharges. If you don’t have sufficient miles in your personal mileage account for a
    flight award, you can still redeem the miles you have earned thus far to pay for part of the cost of the flight.



    Of course, the attractive flight awards in the Miles & More program are still available.


    Cash & Miles is an additional option for redeeming miles. The best part is that you can earn new miles on every
    flight.




    FAQs about Cash & Miles

    • In which countries can I use Cash & Miles?

      • You can use Cash & Miles in the following countries: Algeria, Angola, Australia, Austria, Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Belarus, Belgium, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Canada, China, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Egypt, Equatorial Guinea, Estonia, Finland, France, Georgia, Germany, Greece, Hong Kong, Hungary, Iceland, India, Indonesia, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Japan, Jordan, Kazakhstan, Republic of Korea, Kuwait, Latvia, Lebanon, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malaysia, Malta, Montenegro, Morocco, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Oman, Philippines, Poland, Portugal, Qatar, Romania, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Serbia, Singapore, Slovakia, Slovenia, South Africa, South Korea, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan, Thailand, Tunisia, Turkey, Turkmenistan, Ukraine, United Arab Emirates, United Kingdom, USA, and Vietnam.


    • Why is the Cash & Miles option not being displayed?

      • There may be several reasons for this. Perhaps you have not yet signed into lufthansa.com with your login details, or you have not yet reached the required mileage amount of at least 3,000 award miles. Also, Cash & Miles is not yet available in all markets.


    • Which services (flights, taxes, fees, and surcharges, or upgrades) can I pay for using Cash & Miles?

      • With Cash & Miles you can redeem your award miles for the cost of your flight booking in part or in full. This option is available for all travel classes. The fare is calculated based on the total ticket price, including taxes, fees, and surcharges. Cash & Miles cannot be used to purchase upgrades. Upgrades can be purchased as usual by booking an upgrade directly via Miles & More at www.miles-and-more.com.


    • What is the difference between Cash & Miles and a normal flight award?

      • With the flight awards already available in the Miles & More program, we offer you attractive flight offers for maintaining a constant mileage amount. These awards are highly prized and are greatly valued by Miles & More members. However, the number of available flight awards is limited. Our flight awards are sometimes sold out—particularly at peak travel times such as vacation periods.

        Cash & Miles offers you an additional option of redeeming the award miles you’ve earned in part or in full for flight bookings at lufthansa.com. This reduces the value of your ticket that you have to pay for in cash. Cash & Miles is calculated based on the ticket price on the day of booking. This price is converted into miles.
