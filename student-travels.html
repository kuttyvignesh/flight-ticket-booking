Lufthansa student offer for journeys originating from India



Student fares on bookings to Europe, free additional check-in baggage and more flexibility.


If you make a one-way or round-trip booking from India to Europe, our student fares offer significant savings

Our student fares include two free pieces of check-in baggage; weighing no more than 23kg each

Enjoy flexibility on your travel dates, and the option of a refund against a fee

Student fares are clearly identified as "Student" on the flight selection page

Please note:

Student fares are only offered on bookings from India to Europe. We do not offer student fares to USA / Canada.

Student fares are only offered on flights operated by Lufthansa Group airlines, from Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai only

We regret that student discount and additional baggage allowance cannot be added to previously made bookings>

How to book student fares

To book our student fares, begin by verifying your student status below

Upon successful verification, the page will reload and you will be able to search and book your chosen flights. Please note that student fares can only
be booked from this page

Please remember to book ‘Student’ fares in order to receive the student fare benefits which include 2x23kg baggage allowance

For answers to Frequently Asked Questions regarding student fares, please refer to our⮕ ​​Lufthansa student fares overview. For further support please e-
mail us at generationfly@dlh.de stating where you wish to fly from and to.

For all other queries, please contact our ⮕​Service Centre.



To book our student fares, please verify your student status

Before verifying your student status, please ensure your intended flight search meets the following critera.


1. Departure from Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad or Chennai only
2. Travelling to a destination in Europe onlybr>

If your intended flight search does not meet these criteria, please do not proceed with student verification, as you will not be able to book student fares. You can still make a booking from our ​⮕homepage.

Please Note:

For bookings originating in India, Student fares are only available in Economy Class, to destinations in Europe. Two free pieces of check-in baggage
will be offered.

Student fares are only offered on flights operated by Lufthansa Group airlines, from Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai only.

Student fares are valid for travel until 31 March 2026 only.

Student fares are clearly identified as "Student" on the flight selection page.

Student fares are also offered for one-way bookings from the United Kingdom to India. ​Find out more.

Student fares should only be booked for one student per booking. For groups of multiple students, please make separate bookings.

Passengers must be full-time students aged 16 and above, enrolled in a higher educational institution with a valid student email address and must have a valid identification* as noted below.

Student identification, i.e. *a valid educational institution-issued student identification card or University admission letter, must be presented upon
request at any airport check-in counter or gate. Failure to do so may result in additional collection up to cost of the full published fare.

Name in all documents must appear as per Passport. Name changes are not permitted.

Student fares are entitled to 2 pieces of check-in baggage weighing up to 23kg each. For more information on baggage, please see our ​Baggage
Overview.

We regret that student discount and additional baggage allowance cannot be added to previously made bookings.

Fares are subject to change without notice and are based on the most direct routing to each destination. Fares include applicable taxes, fees, carrier
and airport charges. Additional baggage fees may apply. Seats are limited and may not be available on all days/flights. Other restrictions may apply.



Travel Advisory:

Prior to booking your flight, please ensure that you comply with the current entry and transit regulations for your destination. More information on these
regulations can be found ​here.



Flight Rebooking:

If you wish to reschedule/rebook, or cancel your flight booking, you can do this online by ​logging in to your booking.



When rebooking, please ensure you select the Student fare option in order to keep the student fare benefits such as additional baggge allowance. There
may be a charge if the price if your new flight(s) is higher.

When cancelling a Student fare, a fee may be charged.

If you do not see the option to cancel or rebook a Student fare online, or if you need further clarifications, please contact your local ​​Service Centre for
assistance.
